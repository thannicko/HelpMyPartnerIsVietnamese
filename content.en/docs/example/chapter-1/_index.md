# Chapter 1: Referring to yourself & others.

In English, in most cases "you" & "I" would be enough to begin speaking. This is not so easy in Vietnamese if you want to speak in a natural way. This chapter presents:

* The easy way of doing pronouns (you & I, in Vietnamese)
* The natural way of doing pronouns (discovering different pronouns & when to use what)
